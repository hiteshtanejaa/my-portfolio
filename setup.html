First Workspace

I developed the interest of building my workspace in 2021. So I started with what was already there at home — an HP wireless keyboard and mouse combo, the first pair of wireless peripherals my family got me. I get excited by new gadgets (and bored quickly 😁).

This setup taught me web development, made me a WordPress developer, a Tutor, and where I learned React.js.
